Everything Pictures

This page demonstrates my understanding of digital images and the factors that affect their quality, appearance, and performance on the web. It explores the differences between image dimensions, such as comparing a 3000×4000 pixel image to a 300×400 pixel image, and explains how image size impacts both visual quality and file storage requirements. It also examines what happens to an image's file size when its dimensions are reduced, as well as the strengths and best use cases for common web image formats such as JPG, PNG, and GIF. The page discusses image proportionality and aspect ratio, showing why maintaining the correct proportions is important for preventing distortion and ensuring images display properly across different devices and screen sizes. For this page I used AI to help with the overall layout and design. I could not get the images to line up how I wanted so I made a prompt to help me acjeive that aspect. As far as what I learned from this assignment, I would say that I learned how image sizes and edits affect the overall quality of an image. My biggest challenge was editing and changing the image types.
