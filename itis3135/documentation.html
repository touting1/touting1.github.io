Introduction

The Tenacious Octopus plushie collection is a themed set of soft, colorful octopus plushies created for comfort, decoration, and fun.

Each plushie has its own name, size, color, stock status, and cuteness rating based on popularity.

Collection Name: Tenacious Octopus Plushies
  • This guide explains how the plushie collection is organized and cared for.

Plushie Types

Plushies in the collection come in different sizes such as small, large, and jumbo.

Each size is designed for a different purpose, from desk decorations to large cuddle plushies.

Tiny Tako | Blue | Small | Low Stock
  • Plushie types help customers choose the octopus that best fits their style.

Cuteness Ratings

The cuteness rating shows how popular each plushie is compared to the others in the inventory.

A higher rating means the plushie is one of the most liked or requested items in the collection.

Tenacious Octopus: 3 out of 3 cute points
  • Cuteness ratings make the inventory more fun and easier to compare.

Care Instructions

Octopus plushies should be kept clean, dry, and away from rough surfaces that could damage the fabric.

For small stains, gently spot clean the plushie using a damp cloth and mild soap.

Wash Method: Spot clean only Dry Method: Air dry completely
  • Proper care helps each plushie stay soft and display-ready.

Best Practices

Store plushies in a clean area where they will not be crushed, stained, or exposed to too much sunlight.

When updating inventory, make sure the plushie name, color, size, status, and cuteness rating are accurate.

Inventory Check: Name + Color + Size + Status + Rating
  • Keeping the collection organized makes the Tenacious Octopus theme feel more complete.
