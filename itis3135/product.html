The Tenacious Octopus Café

A seafood restaurant and octopus café where guests enjoy ocean-inspired meals beside large aquarium tanks filled with live octopus friends.

We proudly serve seafood, but octopus is never on the menu. Our octopus residents are part of the experience, not the food.

Meet the Octopus Gang

Tenacious Octopus

The main mascot. He is male, half red and half black, bold, confident, and always watching over the café.

Tiny Tako

Tiny Tako is male, orange, playful, and known for hiding behind coral when guests walk by.

Jumbo Inkie

Jumbo Inkie is female, pink, calm, friendly, and the gentle giant of the aquarium tanks.
