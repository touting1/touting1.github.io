Where to Find Us

Tenacious Octopus Laundry is located in a fictional seaside shopping strip where bubbles float in the air and laundry baskets roll in like waves. The location is designed to feel bright, clean, and welcoming.

Customers can stop in for self-service laundry or leave their clothes with our octopus crew for drop-off care. There is plenty of space for baskets, folding, and waiting while clothes finish.
