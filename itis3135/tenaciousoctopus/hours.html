Hours and Specials

Tenacious Octopus Laundry is open most days so customers can stop by whenever laundry starts piling up. Our hardworking octopus crew keeps the washers spinning from morning until evening, with extra tentacles ready to handle busy weekends and holiday rushes. Whether you have a single basket of clothes or several family-sized loads, our team is prepared to provide quick, dependable service. We strive to keep wait times short so customers can spend less time doing laundry and more time enjoying their day.

We also offer special service times for students, families, and customers who need rush orders. Early mornings are perfect for quiet self-service washing, while afternoons are ideal for convenient drop-off and wash-and-fold service. Evening pickup options are available for customers with busy schedules, and same-day service can often be arranged for urgent laundry needs. No matter when you visit, our friendly octopus crew is committed to providing spotless results, excellent customer service, and a fun, welcoming atmosphere every day of the week.
